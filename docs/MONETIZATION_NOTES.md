# Monetization Considerations

For future reference: which APIs and services are safe to use if you monetize Deenify (paid app, ads, IAP, etc.).

---

## Generally Safe for Commercial Use

| Service | License / Terms |
|---------|-----------------|
| **fawazahmed0/quran-api** | Unlicense (public domain). No commercial restriction. |
| **adhan** (batoulapps) | MIT license. Commercial use allowed. |
| **OpenStreetMap / Overpass** | ODbL. Attribution required; commercial use allowed. |
| **Aladhan API** | Prayer time APIs commonly used in commercial apps. |
| **Google Maps** | Google Maps Platform Terms apply; commercial use allowed under their terms. |

---

## Verify Before Monetizing

| Service | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| **everyayah.com** (Quran audio) | No formal terms. Copyright typically held by reciters. Commercial use uncertain—contact them or reciters' representatives, or use a service with clear commercial licensing. |
| **Hadith API** | Free API; commercial terms not clearly stated. Confirm with hadithapi.com. |
| **UmmahAPI** | Fallback for prayer times; confirm commercial use. |
| **islamcan.com** | Reminder sound; no clear commercial license. Confirm or replace. |

---

## Recommendation

Before monetizing, contact everyayah.com, hadithapi.com, UmmahAPI, and islamcan.com for explicit commercial-use permission. For Quran audio, consider switching to a source with explicit commercial licensing if permission isn't granted.

See `docs/API_CREDITS.md` for full attribution requirements.
